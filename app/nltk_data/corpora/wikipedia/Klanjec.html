Klanjec is a small town in northwestern Croatia, in the region of Hrvatsko Zagorje on the border with Slovenia. It is located at around 46°2′48″N, 15°44′10″E. The population of Klanjec is 562, but there are 3,234 people in the municipality (census 2001). Absolute majority are Croats at 98%.
