Bharat Chipli

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bharat Chipli (Kannada: ಭರತ್ ಚಿಪ್ಲಿ) (Born 27 January 1983 in Sagar, Shimoga, Karnataka) is an Indian cricketer who plays for Karnataka in first-class cricket.

[edit] Career

He studied at BHS College, Jayanagar, Bangalore. He plays for Swastic Union Cricket Club (I) in the KSCA Group, I Division. Chipli, an aggressive opening bat and also a dynamic fielder in the Karnataka Ranji squad. The right-hander is part of the Royal Challengers Bangalore team in the Indian Premier League. Chipli made his debut against the formidable side Chennai Super Kings.
