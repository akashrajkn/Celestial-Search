Lodno

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Kysucké Nové Mesto District in the Žilina Region

Lodno is a village and municipality in Kysucké Nové Mesto District in the Zilina Region of northern Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1658.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 513 metres and covers an area of 8.847km². It has a population of about 991 people.
