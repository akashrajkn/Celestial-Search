KWAI is a radio station located in Honolulu, Hawaii, that is owned by Radio Hawaii, Incorporated. The station offers an All-Talk format, broadcasting at 1080 kHz with ERP of 5 Kw and bills itself on air as "K-108, The Talk of the Town."
