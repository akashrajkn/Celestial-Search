J. David Molson

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

J. David Molson was a President of the Montreal Canadiens from 1964 until 1972. He succeeded Hartland Molson and was succeeded by Jacques Courtois. He won 5 Stanley Cups with Montreal in 1965, 1966, 1968, 1969, and 1971.
