Mahmoud Khordbin (Persian: محمود خوردبین,Born September 2, 1948 in Tehran,Iran ) is an Iranian former football player and Persepolis F.C.'s current Executive Director. He only played for Persepolis F.C. in his entire career and was first ever famous player of Persepolis F.C.. He was member of persepolis since the club was on 2nd Division and played untill 1980.He also has 2 National caps for Team melli. After he retired,he became Executive Director of persepolis football team until 2005 and made an unbreakable record. He was returned to persepolis as Director another time by Habib Kashani in 2007-08 season and then was selected as club's Vice Persident.

after suffering a heart attack in the middle of 2007-08 Season,he continued his job at persepolis only as director.
