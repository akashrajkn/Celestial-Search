Fertit

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Fertit is an ethnic group in the Central African Republic and Sudan. Most members of this ethnic group speak Arabic. Most members of this ethnic group are Muslims. Some members of this ethnic group speak Kara, a Bantu language.
