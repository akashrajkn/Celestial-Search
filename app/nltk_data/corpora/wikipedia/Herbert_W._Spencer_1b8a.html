Herbert W. Spencer

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Herbert Winfield Spencer (April 7, 1905 â€“ September 18, 1992) was a film composer and orchestrator.

He was known mostly for his collaborations with composer, John Williams as orchestrator and arranger (from 1974 until his death) for many of his film scores such as the original Star Wars trilogy, E.T. The Extra-Terrestrial and the Indiana Jones film series.
