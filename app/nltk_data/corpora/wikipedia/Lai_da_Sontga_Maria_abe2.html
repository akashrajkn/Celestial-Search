Lai da Sontga Maria (Italian: Lago di Santa Maria) is a lake near the Lukmanier Pass in the municipality of Medel (Lucmagn), Grisons, Switzerland. The reservoir has a surface area is 1.77 km². The arch dam Santa Maria was completed in 1968.
