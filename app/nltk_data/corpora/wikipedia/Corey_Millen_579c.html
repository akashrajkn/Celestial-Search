Corey Millen

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Corey Millen (born April 29, 1964 in Cloquet, Minnesota, United States) is a retired American ice hockey forward.

[edit] NHL

Millen started his National Hockey League career with the New York Rangers in 1990. He also played for the Dallas Stars, New Jersey Devils, Los Angeles Kings, and Calgary Flames. He left the NHL after the 1997 season.

[edit] Europe

In 1997–98 He joined the Cologne Sharks of the DEL. He played in Cologne until the end of the 2001–02 season. In 2002–03 He joined HC Lugano of the Swiss A League for 1 season before retiring from hockey.
