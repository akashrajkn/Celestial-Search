Mad Locust Rising is the second release from the US speed metal band Agent Steel. It was recorded at Indigo Ranch Studios, Malibu, California and mastered at Frankort Wayne Studios by Tom Coyne. The album was released by Combat Records and Music For Nations in September 1985 and reissused in 1999 by Century Media.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "The Swarm Is Upon Us" – 0:16
  2. "Mad Locust Rising" – 4:15
  3. "The Ripper (Judas Priest Cover)" – 2:33
  4. "Let It Be Done/The Day At Guyana" – 4:40

The cassette version contained the bonus track "Taken By Force".
