Capitán FAP Guillermo Concha Iberico International Airport (IATA: PIU, ICAO: SPUR) is an airport serving Piura, Peru. It is run by CORPAC S.A. a government run organization that oversees the management of Peruvian airports. It is the main airport of the Piura Region, the second most populus of the country. It is currently served by AeroCondor and LAN Peru. It is located 2km from Piura's main square and 130 km from the famous Máncora Resort.
