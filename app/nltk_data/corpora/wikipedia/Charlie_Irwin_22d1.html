Charles Edwin Irwin (February 15, 1869 in Clinton, Illinois - September 21, 1925 in Chicago, Illinois), is a former professional baseball player who played third base in the Major Leagues from 1893-1902. He would play for the Chicago Cubs, Cincinnati Reds, and Brooklyn Dodgers.
