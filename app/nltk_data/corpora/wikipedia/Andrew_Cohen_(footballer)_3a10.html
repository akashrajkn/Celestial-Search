Andrew Cohen (born April 13, 1981 in Paola, Malta) is a Maltese football striker currently playing for Hibernians in Malta. He has 21 international caps for Malta.[1]

He was top scorer with 21 goals in the Maltese Premier League 2004–05 season.[2] And won the prestigious award of Footballer of the Year two times in a row in 2004/2005 and 2005/2006.
