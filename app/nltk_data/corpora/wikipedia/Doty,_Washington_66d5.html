Doty, Washington

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Doty is an Uninc town between Dryad and Pe Ell in Washington State, US.

There is a Post Office and Fire Department and the Chehalis River runs through it.

Coordinates: 46°38′04″N, 123°16′40″W
