Johnston's Genet (Genetta johnstoni) is a species of carnivore mammal, related to civets and linsangs in the family Viverridae. Its range includes the African countries of Côte d'Ivoire, Ghana, Guinea and Liberia. It inhabits the regions rainforests, and the rarely-sighted species is considered one of West Africa's least known small carnivores.
