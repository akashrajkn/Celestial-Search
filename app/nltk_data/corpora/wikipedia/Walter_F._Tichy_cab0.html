Walter F. Tichy

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Walter F. Tichy is professor of computer science at the University of Karlsruhe in Germany where he teaches classes in software engineering.

To the larger software development community he is mostly known as the initial developer of the RCS revision control system.
