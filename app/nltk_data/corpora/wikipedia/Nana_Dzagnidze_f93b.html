Nana Dzagnidze

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Nana Dzagnidze (born 1 January 1987) is a female chess player from Georgia. She holds the FIDE titles of International Master and Woman Grandmaster .

Her best position in the women's top 50 list of FIDE was #11 in October 2005 and January 2006. In September 2005 she took part in the 6th Lausanne Young Masters tournament in Lausanne, finishing seventh. Andrei Volokitin won the tournament.
