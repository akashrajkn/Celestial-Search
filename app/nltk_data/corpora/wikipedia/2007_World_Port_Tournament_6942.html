2007 World Port Tournament

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Official logo

The 2007 World Port Tournament is the 11th edition and will be held in the Neptunus Familie Stadion in Rotterdam, The Netherlands. The tournament takes place from 2 to 12 August 2007.
