List of AL Silver Slugger winners at shortstop

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The following is a list of AL Silver Slugger winners at shortstop. Number of awards won is in parenthesis.

Year Player Team
1980 Robin Yount (1) Milwaukee Brewers
1981 Rick Burleson California Angels
1982 Robin Yount (2) Milwaukee Brewers
1983 Cal Ripken, Jr. (1) Baltimore Orioles
1984 Cal Ripken, Jr. (2) Baltimore Orioles
1985 Cal Ripken, Jr. (3) Baltimore Orioles
1986 Cal Ripken, Jr. (4) Baltimore Orioles
1987 Alan Trammell (1) Detroit Tigers
1988 Alan Trammell (2) Detroit Tigers
1989 Cal Ripken, Jr. (5) Baltimore Orioles
1990 Alan Trammell (3) Detroit Tigers
1991 Cal Ripken, Jr. (6) Baltimore Orioles
1992 Travis Fryman Detroit Tigers
1993 Cal Ripken, Jr. (7) Baltimore Orioles
Year Player Team
1994 Cal Ripken, Jr. (8) Baltimore Orioles
1995 John Valentin Boston Red Sox
1996 Alex Rodriguez (1) Seattle Mariners
1997 Nomar Garciaparra Boston Red Sox
1998 Alex Rodriguez (2) Seattle Mariners
1999 Alex Rodriguez (3) Seattle Mariners
2000 Alex Rodriguez (4) Seattle Mariners
2001 Alex Rodriguez (5) Texas Rangers
2002 Alex Rodriguez (6) Texas Rangers
2003 Alex Rodriguez (7) Texas Rangers
2004 Miguel Tejada (1) Baltimore Orioles
2005 Miguel Tejada (2) Baltimore Orioles
2006 Derek Jeter (1) New York Yankees
2007 Derek Jeter (2) New York Yankees
