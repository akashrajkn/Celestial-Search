Ramon Stagnaro

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ramon Stagnaro, born in Peru, is a guitarist who has toured or recorded with artists such as Diana Ross, Celine Dion, Pedro Eustache, Enrique Iglesias, Nelly Furtado, Andrea Bocelli,Shahyar Ghanbari,Vangelis and appears on Yanni's live concert video, Tribute.
