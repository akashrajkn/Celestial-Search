Thomas Browne (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Thomas Browne may refer to:

  • Thomas Browne (1605-1682), an English author.
  • Thomas Browne, 4th Viscount Kenmare (1726-1795), an Irish landowner and politician.
  • Thomas Alexander Browne (1826-1915), an Australian writer, who sometimes published under the pseudonym Rolf Boldrewood; best known for his novel Robbery Under Arms.
  • Thomas Gore Browne, CB, (1807-1887), Governor of St Helena, Governor of New Zealand, Governor of Tasmania and Governor of Bermuda.
  • Thomas H. B. Browne (1844-1892), a Republican member of the U.S. House of Representatives from Virginia.
  • Thomas M. Browne (1829-1891), a U.S. Representative from Indiana.
