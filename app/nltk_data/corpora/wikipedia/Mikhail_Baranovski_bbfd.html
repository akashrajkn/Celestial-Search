Mikhail Baranovski (Russian: Михаил Барановский: born 4 January 1983) is a Russian footballer. He currently plays for FC Baltika Kaliningrad in Russia. Baranovski played for FC Baltika Kaliningrad in the Russian First Division during the 2006 season.[1]
