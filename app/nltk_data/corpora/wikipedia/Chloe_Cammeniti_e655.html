Chloe Cammeniti is a fictional character in the Australian soap opera Neighbours,.

Chloe was born on the January 21, 2008 , the daughter of Carmella Cammeniti and Oliver Barnes. Her mother and father were fighting over her in a custody battle, but now her father Oliver has dropped the custody case, but still doesn't trust Marco. Chloe's godparents are Rosetta Cammeniti and Toadie Rebecchi.
