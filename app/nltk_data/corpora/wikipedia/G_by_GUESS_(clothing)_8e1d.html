G by Guess is a new division of GUESS? brand. The clothes is best described as independent, bold, confident, alive, and stylish. Known for quality, trendsetting style, and marketing creativity, the Company designs and markets a leading lifestyle collection of casual apparel and accessories for women and men.

[edit] Stores

G by GUESS stores are made to entertain while you shop. Music videos, DJ's and various events are on to have an impression of a night-club atmosphere. The stores are designed with an urban look being "chic" and "sexy".

  • G by GUESS has earned first place in the annual Retail Store of the Year design competition.
