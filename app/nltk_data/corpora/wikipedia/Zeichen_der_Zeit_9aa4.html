Zeichen der Zeit

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Zeichen der Zeit is a music project by various German pop musicians. Their common goal is to express their Christian belief and to make an issue out of it in the secular media. An album of the same title appeared in 2004, and the song "Du bist nicht allein" became a substantial hit. The project continued in 2006 with the second album "David Generation", which has less contributors in order to give it a more distinct sound.
