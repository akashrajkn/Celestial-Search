Nimpo Lake, British Columbia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Nimpo Lake is an unincororporated community and First Nations reserve of the Tsilhqot'in people, located on the east shore of Nimpo Lake in the Chilcotin District of the Central Interior of British Columbia, Canada. It is located on BC Highway 37. Coordinates: 52°20′00″N, 125°09′00″W
