The Three Words to Remember in Dealing with the End is the debut EP by Maryland pop punk band All Time Low, released in 2004 through label Emerald Moon Records.

[edit] Track listing

  1. "Hit the Lights"
  2. "The Next Best Thing"
  3. "Last Flight Home"
  4. "Memories that Fade like Photographs"
