Abraxas (Czech band)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abraxas is Czech rock band, formed in 1976. The leader is guitarist and composer Slávek Janda.

[edit] Present members

  • Slávek Janda, g, voc
  • Miroslav Imrich, voc
  • Ivan Pelíšek, dr
  • Zdeněk Chládek, bs

[edit] Discography

  • Tak pojď / Školník - SP- 1977, Panton
  • Cesta jinam 1 / Cesta jinam 2 - SP - 1977,
  • Box - LP- 1982, Panton (the most successful record of the band)
  • Šťastnej blázen - LP - 1986, Panton
  • Manéž - LP - 1984, Panton
  • Midnight City - LP - 1985, Artia
  • The Best Of… (1979 - 1982) - 1994, B&M Music
  • Sado - Maso - 1996, Forte/Happy Music (reedice 2002, Master Music s.r.o.)
  • Box & 10x bonus (reedition on CD) - 2000, Sony Music/Bonton
  • Rituál 2000, Sony Music/Bonton
  • Retrospektiva, 2003
  • Už je to jedno, 2007
