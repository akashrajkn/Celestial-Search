The Caribbean Cruise Caper

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Caribbean Cruise Caper is the 154th book The Hardy Boys Mystery Stories by Franklin W. Dixon.

[edit] Plot summary

The Hardy boys go on a cruise to sail in the Caribbean. But things don't go right when a suspicious character goes off and creates havoc in the cruise. Now, the Hardy Boys must get to the bottom of the case, and find out who is causing the havoc.
