Charles-François-Prosper Guérin

Charles-François-Prosper Guérin (Sens, 1875-1939) was a French post-impressionist painter. He studied with Gustave Moreau in the l'École des Beaux Arts à Paris, and mad one exhibition in Grafton Galleries. He was Jean Michel Prosper Guérin’s son.[1].
