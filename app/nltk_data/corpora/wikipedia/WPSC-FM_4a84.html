WPSC-FM (88.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Alternative format. Licensed to Wayne, New Jersey, USA, the station serves the New Jersey college area. The station is currently owned by William Paterson College of N. J..[2]

The station is a part of the Communication department and is staffed by students interested in careers in radio. The station began operating in December 1988. [3]
