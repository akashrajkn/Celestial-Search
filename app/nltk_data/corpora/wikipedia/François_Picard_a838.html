François Picard (born April 26, 1921, - died April 29, 1996 [1]) was a racing driver from France. He participated in one Formula One Grand Prix, on October 19, 1958. He scored no championship points. This race was his last, as he crashed his Cooper into Olivier Gendebien's Ferrari, which had spun in front of him, and Picard suffered serious injuries. He eventually recovered, but never raced again.
