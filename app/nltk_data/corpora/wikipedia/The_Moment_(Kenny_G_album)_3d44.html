The Moment is the seventh studio album by saxophonist Kenny G. It was released by Arista Records in 1996, and reached number 1 on the Contemporary Jazz Albums chart, number 2 on the Billboard 200, number 9 on the R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart and number 16 on the Canadian Albums chart.[1]
