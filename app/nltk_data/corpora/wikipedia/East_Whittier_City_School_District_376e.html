East Whittier City School District

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

East Whittier City School District is a school district based in Whittier, California, United States.

EWCSD serves a large portion of the city of Whittier, and is comprised of Elementary and [[Middle School|Middle] schools. The city's secondary schools are encompassed by the Whittier Union High School District.
