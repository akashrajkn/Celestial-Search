2004 Malaysian motorcycle Grand Prix

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The 2004 Malaysian motorcycle Grand Prix was the fourteenth round of the 2004 MotoGP Championship. It took place on the weekend of 8th-10th October, 2004 at the Sepang International Circuit.
