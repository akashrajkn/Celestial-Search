[edit] WarCry Line-ups

(2001-2002)
WarCry
  • Víctor García - lead vocals
  • Pablo García - guitars (guest)
  • Fernando Mon - guitars (guest)
  • Alberto Ardines - drums
(2002-2004)
El Sello De Los Tiempos,
Alea Jacta Est
  • Víctor García - lead vocals
  • Pablo García - guitars
  • Fernando Mon - guitars
  • Alvaro Jardón - bass
  • Manuel Ramil - keyboards
  • Alberto Ardines - drums
(2004-2007)
¿Dónde Está La Luz?,
Directo A La Luz (live),
La Quinta Esencia
  • Víctor García - lead vocals
  • Pablo García - guitars
  • Fernando Mon - guitars
  • Roberto García - bass
  • Manuel Ramil - keyboards
  • Alberto Ardines - drums
(2007-2008)
  • Víctor García - lead vocals
  • Pablo García - guitars
  • José Rubio - guitars
  • Roberto García - bass
  • Manuel Ramil - keyboards
  • Rafael Yugueros - drums
(2008-present)
  • Víctor García - lead vocals
  • Pablo García - guitars
  • José Rubio - guitars
  • Roberto García - bass
  • Rafael Yugueros - drums
