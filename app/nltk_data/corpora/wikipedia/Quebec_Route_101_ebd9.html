Route 101 is a highway in northwestern Quebec running in a north south direction.

In the north the highway begins at Route 111 in Macamic and ends at Témiscaming, a town bordering with Thorne, Ontario on the Ottawa River. It continues as Highway 63 to North Bay, Ontario.
