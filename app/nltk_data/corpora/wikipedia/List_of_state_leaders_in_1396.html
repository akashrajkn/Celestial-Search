[edit] Africa

  • Ethiopia - Dawit I, Emperor of Ethiopia, (1382 - 1413)

[edit] Asia

  • China (Ming Dynasty) - Hongwu, Emperor of China (1368 - 1398)
  • Chūzan - Bunei - King of Chūzan (1395-1407)
  • Hokuzan - Min - King of Hokuzan (1396-1400)
  • Japan
    • Monarch - Go-Komatsu, Emperor of Japan (1392-1412)
    • Shogun (Ashikaga) - Ashikaga Yoshimochi, Shogun of Japan (1394-1423)
  • Korea (Joseon Dynasty) - Taejo, King of Joseon (1392-1398)
  • Nanzan - Monarch
  • Multiple rival claims to the title.
    1. Ofusato - King of Nanzan (1337-1396)
    2. Oueishi - King of Nanzan (1388-1402)

[edit] Europe

  • Achaea -
    1. interregnum (1386 - 1396)
    2. Pedro Bordo, Prince of Achaea (1396 - 1402)
  • Bulgarian Empire - Ivan Sratsimir, Tsar of Bulgaria (1356-1396)
  • Byzantine Empire - Manuel II Palaeologus, Byzantine Emperor, (1391-1425)
  • Castile - Henry III, King of Castile (1390-1406)
  • England - Richard II, King of England (1377-1399)
  • France - Charles VI, King of France (1380-1422)
  • Germany - (House of Luxembourg) - Wenceslaus, King of the Romans (1378-1400)
    • Bavaria-Straubing (House of Wittelsbach) - Albert, Duke of Bavaria-Straubing and count of Holland and Hainaut (1347-1404)
  • Moldavia - Stephen I, Prince of Moldavia (1394-1399)
  • Naples - Louis II, King of Naples (1389 - 1399)
  • Portugal - John I, King of Portugal (1385-1433)
  • Taranto - Raimondo del Balzo Orsini, Prince of Taranto (1393 - 1406)
  • Wallachia - Mircea I, Prince of Wallachia (1386-1418)

[edit] Middle East and North Africa

  • Cyprus - James I, King of Cyprus (1382-1398)
  • Egypt - Barquq, Sultan of Egypt (1390-1399)
  • Ottoman Empire - Beyazid I, Sultan of the Ottoman Empire (1389-1402)
