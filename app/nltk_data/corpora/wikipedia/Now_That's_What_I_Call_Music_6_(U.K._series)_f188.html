Now That's What I Call Music 6 (U.K. series)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article describes the 6th album in the UK Now! series. It should not be confused with the 2001 US album of the same name. For more information, see Now That's What I Call Music! 6 (U.S. series).

Now That's What I Call Music 6 or Now 6 was released in 1985. The album is the 6th edition of the (UK) Now! series. It made it to #1 in the UK initially for two weeks, then returned for another two weeks in January 1986 after Now - The Christmas Album had replaced it at the top. It was also the first Now album to go 4x platinum, selling over 1.2 million copies.
