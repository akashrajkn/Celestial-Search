The Crack magazine is a free style and listings magazine. Published monthly in print and online, it covers entertainment and culture for the North East region of England. It provides comprehensive listings and previews on music, art, film, dance, comedy, theater and clubs.

As well as newsagents throughout the North-East, the Crack is also available from cultural venues, pubs, restaurants and galleries.

Its office is in Pink Lane in Newcastle upon Tyne, near Newcastle Central station.
