Malnaş or Málnás (Romanian: Malnaş; Hungarian: Málnás) is a commune in Covasna County, Romania comprising 2 villages:

  • Lutoasa/Csomortán
  • Mereni/Kézdialmás

[edit] Demographics

The commune has an absolute Székely Hungarian majority. It became a commune in its own right along with the village of Lutoasa (Csomortán) in 2004 when it split from Lemnia. For demographics see Lemnia.
