What does the "meh" mean on MLIA?


Me or Eh...
"Meh" is an expression showing that something is mediocre. In this case, "meh" is used to show that you didn't think the post was very good.

An example of a meh-worthy post: "Today, I woke up and got out of bed. MLIA"

An example of an average-worthy post: "Today, I woke up and got out of bed, only to find my friends in my room with two giant cakes. I don't know what the occasion is, but I don't care. MLIA"
