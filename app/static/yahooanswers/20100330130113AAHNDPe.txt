This link looks like it will get you taken care of:

http://www.google.com/support/accounts/bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=61416

It shows you to make sure your cookies are enabled and if they are they offered the following advice:

If you've already checked that your cookies are enabled and you're still seeing an error message that your browser's cookie functionality is turned off, try opening a new window in your browser. Sometimes using tabs in a single browser instance can cause your cookies to stick.

Still receiving this error message? Clear your cache and delete your cookies.
http://www.google.com/support/accounts/bin/answer.py?answer=32050
