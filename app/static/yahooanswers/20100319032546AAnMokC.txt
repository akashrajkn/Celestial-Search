How do i turn off firefox word fixer spelling mistake error thing?
The red underline of most words gets very annoying...

Tools -> Add-ons -> Find the dictionary in the list of addons and click Disable.
Do this:

1.Click the Tools->Options menu item.
2.Click the Advanced Page.
3.Click the General tab.
4.Uncheck the Check my spelling as I type checkbox.
5.Click OK.

Hope that helps.
