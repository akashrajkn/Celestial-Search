Blocking pop-ups on Safari?
How block the pop ups on Safari.When i used IE I never got pop ups now I do.Also when my sister uses Safari I have never seen her have pop ups.

Click the icon that looks like a flower next to the icon the looks like a piece of paper.
There will be a menu that comes up
Hide Menu Bar/Show Menu Bar
Hide Bookmarks Bar/Show Bookmarks Bar
Hide Status Bar/Show Status Bar
Hide Tab Bar/Show Tab Bar
Customize Toolbar
History
Downloads
Private Browsing
Reset Safari
Block Pop-up Windows
Preferences
Help
Report Bugs To Apple
About Safari

For future reference just click Show Menu Bar then on the menu bar click edit and then click Block Pop-up Windows.
