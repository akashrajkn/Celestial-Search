Decahedron

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the Washington, DC post-hardcore band, see Decahedron

A decahedron is a polyhedron with 10 faces. Decahedrons made out of regular polygons include:

  • Pentagonal dipyramid
  • Octagonal prism
  • Square antiprism
  • Square cupola

A very important decahedron, for its use in role playing games to simulate probabilities, is the Pentagonal trapezohedron, usually known by d10.
