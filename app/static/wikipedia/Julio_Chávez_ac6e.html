Julio Chávez

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Chavez in El Custodio

Julio Chávez (born July 14, 1956 in Buenos Aires, Argentina) is a film and television actor.

He works in the cinema of Argentina.[1]

[edit] Filmography (partial)

  • El Visitante (1999)
  • La Sombra (2002)
  • Un Oso Rojo (2002) aka A Red Bear
  • Dormir al Sol (2003)
  • El Custodio (2006) aka The Minder
  • El Otro (2007), aka The Other

[edit] Television (partial)

  • De Poeta y de Loco (1996) (TV Series)
  • Archivo Negro (1997) (mini TV Series)
  • Epitafios (2004) (mini TV Series) aka Epitaphs

[edit] Awards

Wins

  • Argentine Film Critics Association Awards: Silver Condor, Best Actor, for El Custodio; Best Actor, for A Red Bear; 2003.
  • Lleida Latin-American Film Festival: Best Actor, for A Red Bear; 2003.
  • Bogota Film Festival: Honorable Mention, Best Actor, for El Custodio; 2006.
  • Berlin International Film Festival: Silver Bear, Best Actor, for El Otro; 2007.
  • Association of Latin Entertainment Critics Awards: Best Actor, for El Custodio; 2008.

Nominations

  • Argentine Film Critics Association Awards: Silver Condor, Best Actor, for El Visitante; (2000).
  • Argentine Film Critics Association Awards: Silver Condor, Best Actor, for Extraño; (2005).
