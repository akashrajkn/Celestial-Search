Bellerive Elementary School

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bellerive Elementary School is an elementary school located in Creve Coeur, Missouri. It is part of the St. Louis County Parkway School District.

The school first opened its doors in 1968. In 1992 it was named a Missouri Gold Star School and a United States U.S. Blue Ribbon School. [1] The school mascot is the roadrunner. As of 2006, the school principal is Debbie Blount. Average enrollment at the school has fluctuated from 470 to 493 students from 2001-2005. Of those, between 13.1% and 19% qualified for the free or reduced lunch program. [2]
