Tom Buckner

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Thomas ("Tom") Christopher Buckner (born 16/04/1963) is an English long distance runner who finished 5th at the 1994 Commonwealth Games in Victoria, Canada, as well as winning the AAA steeplechase on two occasions. He is the younger brother of Jack Buckner who was also an international athlete. Both of the Buckners attended Worksop College, a public school located in North Nottinghamshire.

  • 1500 metres - 3:42.85 (1992)

  • 3000 metres steeplechase - 8:25.50 (1992)
  • 5000 metres - 14.02:93 (1994)
  • 10000 metres (road) - 29:39.00 (1994)
  • Half marathon - 65:13 (1993)
  • Marathon - 2:21.40 (1995)
