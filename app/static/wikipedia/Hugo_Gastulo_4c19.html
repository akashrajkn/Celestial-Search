Alejandro Hugo Gastulo Ramírez (born 9 January 1958) is a retired Peruvian professional football defender.

He competed for the Peru national football team at the 1982 FIFA World Cup. At club level he played for Universitario de Deportes and Alianza Lima in Peru.
