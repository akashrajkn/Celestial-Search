Travma is the name of a Greek album by singer Anna Vissi. It was released in Greece and Cyprus on April 14, 1997 where it reached 3X Platinum status. It also included a duet with singer Sakis Rouvas.

[edit] Music

Music and lyrics are by Nikos Karvelas and Natalia Germanou


The album went #1 in Greece and Cyprus and #73 in Australian ARIA Album Chart.
