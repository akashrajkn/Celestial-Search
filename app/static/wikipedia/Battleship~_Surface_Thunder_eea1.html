Battleship: Surface Thunder is a PC video game by Hasbro Interactive. It was released in the United States on September 12, 2000. The game is split into two modes, one being the regular classic battleship, and the other being a new arcade version.

[edit] Reviews

The game currently has a 7.4/10 (Decent) rating on IGN. Gamespot and CNET both rated Battleship: Surface Thunder a 5.0/10. Many praised the classic version mode and detailed ships and environments, but criticized repetitive gameplay and some visual issues.
