Hatfield Broad Oak

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Hatfield Broad Oak (or Hatfield Regis[1]) is a village and civil parish in the Uttlesford district of Essex, England, about five and a half miles south-east of Bishop's Stortford. Each May Bank holiday a 10 kilometre road race is held using a course running around the village.[2]

According to the 2001 census it had a population of 1,600.[3]
