KDOX is a commercial radio station located in Henderson, Nevada, broadcasting to the Las Vegas metropolitan area on 1280 AM. KDOX airs News/Talk programming branded as "Fox News 1280." The station is currently owned by S & R Broadcasting, Inc and features programing from Fox News Radio, Premiere Radio Networks and Talk Radio Network.[1]

[edit] History

The station went on the air as KREL on February 17, 1984. On January 28, 1991, the station changed its call sign to KDOL, and on March 6, 1998 to the current KDOX.[2]
