Bulangaa, Töv

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Bulangaa (Mongolian: Булангаа) is a settlement in Büren sum of Töv Province in Mongolia. Bulangaa settlement is former Önjüül sum center. It is 43 km SE from Büren sum center and 50 km SW from Bayan-Önjüül sum center.
