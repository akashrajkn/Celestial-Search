Shimerville, Pennsylvania

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Shimerville is an unincorporated community in the U.S. state of Pennsylvania located on Pennsylvania Route 100 and Pennsylvania Route 29. Shimmerville is located in Upper Milford Township in Lehigh County. The community is located just outside and to the south of Macungie, Pennsylvania and to the west of Vera Cruz, Pennsylvania.
