Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Jalan Arang (S.M.K. Jalan Arang or SMK Jalan Arang) is a secondary school situated on Jalan Arang in Kuching, Sarawak, Malaysia. Hence, the name of the school. The school started it's operations in January 2003.

Built on a 16-acre site, the school is complete with 38 classrooms, a staffroom, administrative offices, a canteen, library, science labs, computer labs, basketball courts, a futsal court, a tennis court, a football field and a hall currently in construction.

[edit] History

SMK Jalan Arang had it's humble new beginnings in the year 2003, pioneered by Mdm. Belinda Lim Sok Khoon (who had left for SMB St.Joseph, Kuching) as the Acting Principal and Mdm. Hii Kwong Ing as the Senior Assistant of Student Affairs along with 878 students, 43 academic staff and 7 non-academic staff. During that time, 22 classes were assigned (from transition class until Form 4). Due to the small amounts of students, there were only morning session for the class schedule.

In 2004, an afternoon session was added, thus making SMK Jalan Arang a two-session school (morning and afternoon) to accommodate the expansion into 31 classes for 1221 students, 59 academic staff and 11 non-academic staff. The year 2004 also marks the arrival of Mdm. Mary John as the principal of SMK Jalan Arang . Mr. Daniel Anyie Eng was appointed the Afternoon Session Supervisor while Mr. William Tedong as the Senior Assistant in Co-Curricular Activities.

In July 2005, Mr. Haris Fadzillah Hj. Bahar was transferred over from SMK Matang Jaya as Senior Assistant in Administration, replacing Mdm. Belinda Lim, who had left SMK Jalan Arang a month earlier for SMB St.Joseph, Kuching.

In July 2006, Mdm. Mary John, then principal of SMK Jalan Arang, was transferred to SMK St. Teresa. Then in October, Mdm. Fong Yut Kuen arrived and was appointed the principal of SMK Jalan Arang until today.

In 2007, construction commenced on a school hall over the school's futsal court and was completed in the same year.

[edit] Vision

Sekolah berwibawa dan unggul dalam menghasilkan

pelajar yang maju dan berjaya serta berakhlak mulia,

selaras dengan keperluan negara.

[edit] Mission

  • Pengajaran dan pembelajaran yang berkualiti, dan terkini.
  • Pengurusan sumber serta kemudahan yang cekap dan efisien.
  • Penyediaan budaya sekolah yang penyayang, ceria, selesa dan selamat.
  • Penglibatan aktif dan penyediaan latihan cemerlang dalam kegiatan ko-kurikulum.
