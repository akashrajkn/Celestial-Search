Theme Time Radio Hour: The Devil

Episode Name: Devil

Air Date: August 2, 2006
Previous episode: Rich Man, Poor Man
Next episode: Eyes

Theme Time Radio Hour (TTRH) is a one hour radio show hosted by Bob Dylan. Each episode explores a different theme. Devil is the 14th episode from season one which ran from May 3, 2006 to April 18, 2007 on XM Satellite Radio for a total of 50 shows. This episode first aired on August 2, 2006.
