WYFA is a Non-Commercial Religious station in Augusta, Georgia owned by the Bible Broadcasting Network. The station is licensed by the FCC to Waynesboro, and broadcast on 107.1 FM with a ERP of 25kW.

[edit] Station History

WYFA signed on March 4th, 1994 with its current format.

Interestingly enough, this wasn't the first time that WYFA was used in the Augusta market as 100.9 WTHB-FM (also licensed to Waynesboro) originally signed on as WYFA in 1988 with the same format.
