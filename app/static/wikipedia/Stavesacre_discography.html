This is a comprehensive discography of Stavesacre, a Fresno, California based Christian hard rock band. As of April 2006 the band has released five studio albums, three extended plays, one compilation, and one DVD. This list does not include material performed by members or former members of Stavesacre that was recorded with The Crucified and Neon Horse.
