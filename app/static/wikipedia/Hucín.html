Hucín

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Revúca District in the Banská Bystrica Region

Hucín (German: Hutzendorf; Hungarian: Gice) is a village and municipality in Revúca District in the Banská Bystrica Region of Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records, the village was first mentioned in 1327 (1327 Gyuche, 1418 Gyche, 1565 Hwczyn, 1566 Huczin Dorf, 1580 Hwsin). t belonged to local feudatories Gizey. Some Germans established here in the XVI century. From 1938 to 1945 it was annexed by Hungary.
