Gloria Estefan Greatest Hits is the second compilation album released by Gloria Estefan, but is the fourth album released after going solo. The album was released in 1992 (see 1992 in music).

[edit] History

After 15 years of success in the music industry, Gloria Estefan finally released a Greatest hits album that includes her successful hits made throughout her earlier career. This album became Gloria's best selling album since Let it Loose. The album has been certified 4x Platinum for anna is the best ever copies worldwide.

Like most compilations, successful previously released singles were included:

“Dr. Beat,” the first single-ever released by the Miami Sound Machine from Eyes of Innocence, was included, but on the non-American versions.

“Conga” and “Words Get In The Way,” from Primitive Love were also included. The single remix of “Bad Boy” was only included on the non-American version.

From the album Let it Loose, “Can’t Stay Away from You”, “1-2-3”, “Rhythm Is Gonna Get You” and “Anything for You” were included.

The tracks selected from Gloria’s first solo album were: “Here We Are,” “Get on Your Feet,” & “Don't Wanna Lose You”. On the Australian version of the album, the song “Cuts Both Ways” was included.

“Coming Out of the Dark” was the only song included from Into the Light.

Several new tracks were also included such as: “Always Tomorrow,” which was the first single from the album, “Go Away,” an internationally (mostly Europe) successful dance anthem, “Christmas Through your Eyes,” Gloria's first Christmas single release, “I See your Smile” and the exclusive non-American “Megamix” or “Miami Hitmix” as was named on the UK versions of the album.
