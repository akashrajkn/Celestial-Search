Disciple (Special Edition) is a special re-release of the sixth album released by Christian rock group Disciple, on June 6, 2006. It contains four bonus songs and a making-of DVD. When the single The Wait is Over was released, it broke several records in Christian music, including the longest spot at No. 1 on the R&R Charts. "Into Black" is now the biggest hit of the album. It is released as a DualDisc album.
