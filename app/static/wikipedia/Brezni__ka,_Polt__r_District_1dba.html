Breznička, Poltár District

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Poltár District in the Banská Bystrica Region

Breznička (Hungarian: Ipolyberzence) is a village and municipality in the Poltár District in the Banská Bystrica Region of Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1279 (Berzenche) as a Slavic settlement.
