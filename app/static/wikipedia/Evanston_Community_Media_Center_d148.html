Evanston Community Media Center

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Evanston Community Media Center or "ECMC" is the designated public access television organization for Evanston, Illinois. ECMC is a "PEG" (public, education, and government) access center which manages and "cablecasts" content produced by the Evanston community, staff and its member organizations.
