The Teacher Training College of Bielsko-Biala is an educational institution in Poland. Established in 1991, the Teacher Training College in Bielsko-Biała continues more than century-old traditions of teacher training in the city and region. It is the largest teacher training college in the country. Currently it trains and educates students in five specialities:

  • Polish language, science information and librarianship,
  • early education,
  • mathematics with informatics,
  • special education - rehabilitation pedagogy with elements of prevention,
  • special education - oligophrenopedagogy.

All the education is done under the academic supervision of well-known universities:

  • Jagiellonian University (in Kraków) [1]
  • Pedagogical Academy (in Kraków) [2]
  • University of Silesia (in Katowice) [3]
  • Jan Dlugosz Academy (in Czestochowa) [4]

Graduates receive at once a College graduation diploma and BA (licencjat) degree awarded by either of the supervising universities. This allows them to continue their MA studies there.
