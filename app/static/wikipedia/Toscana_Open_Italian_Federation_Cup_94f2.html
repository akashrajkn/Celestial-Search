Toscana Open Italian Federation Cup

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Toscana Open Italian Federation Cup is a golf tournament on the Challenge Tour. It will be played for the first time in 2007.

[edit] Tournament hosts

  • 2007 Le Pavoniere Golf & Country Club in Florence, Italy.

[edit] Winners

Toscana Open Italian Federation Cup

  • 2007 Mikael Lundberg
