Space Mountain

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Space Mountain is the name of five different attractions at Disney parks. The name can refer to:

  • Space Mountain (Magic Kingdom) (1975), at Magic Kingdom at the Walt Disney World Resort, in Orlando, Florida.
  • Space Mountain (Disneyland) (1977), at Disneyland in Anaheim, California.
  • Space Mountain (Tokyo Disneyland) (1983), at Tokyo Disneyland in Urayasu, Japan.
  • Space Mountain - Mission 2 (Parc Disneyland) (1995), at Parc Disneyland in Marne La Vallee, France.
  • Space Mountain (Hong Kong Disneyland) (2005), at Hong Kong Disneyland at the Hong Kong Disneyland Resort, in Lantau Island, Hong Kong.
