Sherman Corner, Minnesota

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sherman Corner is a community in St. Louis County, Minnesota, USA.

The community is located south of Cook at the junction of U.S. Highway 53, Minnesota Highway 1, and County Road 22.

Sherman Corner is located within Angora Township.
