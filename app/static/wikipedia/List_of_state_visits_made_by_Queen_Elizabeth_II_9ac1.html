[edit] 1950s

  • November 1953: Republic of Panama, visiting President José Antonio Remón Cantera
  • 24-26 June 1955: Kingdom of Norway, visiting His Majesty King Haakon VII
  • 8-10 June 1956: Kingdom of Sweden, visiting His Majesty King Gustaf VI Adolf
  • 18-21 February 1957: Portuguese Republic, visiting President Craveiro Lopes
  • 8-11 April 1957: French Republic, visiting President René Coty
  • 21-23 May 1957: Kingdom of Denmark, visiting His Majesty King Frederik IX
  • 17-21 October 1957: United States of America, as Queen of Canada, visiting President Dwight David Eisenhower
  • 25-27 March 1958: Kingdom of the Netherlands, visiting Her Majesty Queen Juliana

[edit] 1960s

  • 26 February to 1 March 1961: Nepal, visiting King Mahendra
  • 2-6 March 1961: Iran, visiting Shah Pahlavi
  • 2-5 May 1961: Italy, visiting President Gronchi
  • 5 May 1961: Holy See, visiting Pope John XXIII
  • 23 November 1961: Liberia, visiting President Tubman
  • 1-8 February 1965: Ethiopia, visiting Emperor Haile Selassie I
  • 8-12 February 1965: Sudan, visiting President Dr. Tijani al-Mahi
  • 18-28 May 1965: West Germany, visiting President Lübke
  • 9-13 May 1966: Belgium, visiting King Baudouin I
  • 5-11 November 1968: Brazil, visiting President Costa e Silva
  • 11-18 November 1968: Chile, visiting President Frei
  • 5-10 May 1969: Austria, visiting President Jonas

[edit] 1970s

  • 18-25 October 1971: Turkey, visiting President Sunay
  • 10-15 February 1972: Thailand, visiting King Bhumibol Adulyadej
  • 13-14 March 1972: Maldives, visiting President Nasir
  • 15-19 May 1972: France, visiting President Pompidou
  • 17-21 October 1972: Yugoslavia, visiting President Tito
  • 15-22 March 1974: Indonesia, visiting President Suharto
  • 24 February - 1 March 1975: Mexico, visiting President Echeverría
  • 7-12 May 1975: Japan, visiting Emperor Hirohito
  • 25-28 May 1976: Finland, visiting President Kekkonen
  • 6-11 July 1976: United States of America, visiting President Ford
  • 8-12 November 1976: Luxembourg, visiting Grand Duke Jean
  • 10 February 1977: Samoa, visinting Malietoa Tanumafili II
  • 22-26 May 1978: West Germany, visiting President Scheel
  • 12-13 February 1979: Kuwait, visiting Emir Jaber III
  • 14-16 February 1979: Bahrain, visiting Emir Isa
  • 21-23 February 1979: Qatar, visiting Emir Khalifa
  • 24-26 February 1979: United Arab Emirates, visiting Emir Zayed
  • 27-28 February 1979: Oman, visitin Sultan Qaboos
  • 29 February-1 March 1979: Saudi Arabia, visiting King Khalid
  • 16-19 May 1979: Denmark, visiting Queen Margrethe II

[edit] 1980s

  • 29 April - 2 May 1980: Switzerland, visiting President Chevallaz
  • 14-17 October 1980: Italy, visiting President Pertini
  • 17 October 1980: Holy See, visiting Pope John Paul II
  • 21-23 October 1980: Tunisia, visiting President Bourguiba
  • 25-27 October 1980: Algeria, visiting President Chadli
  • 27-30 October 1980: Morocco, visiting King Hassan II
  • 5-8 May 1981: Norway, visiting King Olav V
  • March 1983: United States of America, visiting President Reagan
  • 25-28 May 1983: Sweden, visiting King Carl XVI Gustaf
  • 26-30 March 1984: Jordan, visiting King Hussein
  • 25-29 March 1985: Portugal, visiting President António Ramalho Eanes
  • 17-20 February 1986: Nepal, visiting King Birendra
  • 12-18 October 1986: China, visiting President Li Xiannian
  • 17-21 October 1988: Spain, visiting King Juan Carlos I

[edit] 1990s

  • 25-27 June 1990: Iceland, visiting President Vigdis Finnbogadóttir
  • 14-17 May 1991: United States of America, visiting President George H. W. Bush
  • 9-12 June 1992: France, visiting President Mitterrand
  • 19-23 October 1992: Germany, visiting President von Weizsäcker
  • 4-7 May 1993: Hungary, visiting President Göncz
  • 17-20 October 1994: Russia, visiting President Yeltsin
  • 25-27 March 1996: Poland, visiting President Kwaśniewski
  • 27-29 March 1996: Czech Republic, visiting President Havel
  • 28 October - 1 November 1996: Thailand, visiting King Bhumibol Adulyadej
  • 19-22 April 1999: South Korea, visiting President Kim Dae-jung

[edit] 2000s

  • 16-19 October 2000: Italian Republic, visiting President Carlo Ciampi
  • 17 October 2000: Holy See, visiting His Holiness Pope John Paul II
  • 30 May - 1 June 2001: Kingdom of Norway, visiting His Majesty King Harald V
  • 5 - 7 April 2004: French Republic, visiting President Jacques Chirac
  • 2 - 4 November 2004: Federal Republic of Germany, visiting President Horst Köhler
  • 17-18 October 2006: Republic of Lithuania, visiting President Valdas Adamkus
  • 18-19 October 2006: Republic of Latvia, visiting President Vaira Vike-Freiberga
  • 19-20 October 2006: Republic of Estonia, visiting President Thomas Ilves
  • 3-8 May 2007: United States of America, visiting President George Walker Bush
  • 21–23 November 2007: Republic of Uganda, visiting President Yoweri Museveni
  • 13-16 May 2008: Republic of Turkey, visiting President Abdullah Gül
