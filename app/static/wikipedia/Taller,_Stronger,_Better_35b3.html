"Taller, Stronger, Better" is a song written by Guy Sebastian, Gary Pinto and Phil Turcio and is the first single off Sebastian's upcoming third studio album, Closer to the Sun. He performed the song in the finale of It Takes Two and the Top 24 show of Australian Idol before the single was released. The single debuted at number three in the ARIA Charts, and remained in the top ten for six weeks.
