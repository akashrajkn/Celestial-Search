Jacques Chastanié

Olympic medal record
Men's athletics
Silver 1900 Paris 5000 metre team race
Bronze 1900 Paris 2500 metre steeplechase

Jacques Chastanié was an early twentieth century French middle-distance athlete who specialised in 2500 metres steeplechase. He participated in Athletics at the 1900 Summer Olympics in Paris and won the Bronze medal in the 2500 metre event. He also won the silver medal in the 5000 metre team race for the French distance team, as well as taking fourth place in the 4000 metre steeplechase.
