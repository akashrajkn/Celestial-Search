Botswana Cricket Association

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Botswana Cricket Association is the official governing body of the sport of cricket in Botswana. Its current headquarters is in Gaborone, Botswana. Botswana Cricket Association is Botswana's representative at the International Cricket Council and has been an associate member of that body since 2005. It is also a member of the African Cricket Association.
