The 2008 Liège-Bastogne-Liège monument classic cycling race took place on April 27, 2008 and was won by Spaniard Alejandro Valverde of Caisse d'Epargne, beating Italian Davide Rebellin of Gerolsteiner and Luxembourger Fränk Schleck of Team CSC in a sprint finish. It was the 94th running of the Liège-Bastogne-Liège, organised by Amaury Sport Organisation and the Royal Pesant Club Liégeois.
