[edit] Notes

  • Pole position: Juan Manuel Fangio - 2:46.5
  • Fastest Lap: Stirling Moss - 2:46.9
  • Lap Leaders:
    • Juan Manuel Fangio 49 laps (1-7 and 9-50)
    • Stirling Moss 1 lap (8)
  • Grand Prix debut for: Luigi Piotti. Only Grand Prix appearance for Jean Lucas.
  • Last Grand Prix appearance for: Nino Farina, John Fitch, Karl Kling, Roberto Mieres, Jacques Pollet, and Ken Wharton.
  • Career Firsts: Carlos Menditeguy (points).
