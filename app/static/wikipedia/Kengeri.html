Coordinates: 12°54′N 77°29′E﻿ / ﻿12.9, 77.48 Kengeri is a suburb of Bangalore in the Indian state of Karnataka.

[edit] Geography

Kengeri is located at 12.9° N 77.48° E[1]. It has an average elevation of 826 metres (2709 feet).

[edit] Demographics

As of 2001 India census[2], Kengeri had a population of 42,386. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Kengeri has an average literacy rate of 75%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 79%, and female literacy is 70%. In Kengeri, 11% of the population is under 6 years of age.
