Ratwara

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ratwara a village in Sitamarhi district of Bihar, India also known as Vishnupur Ratwara. This village is about 20KM east of Sitamarhi district headquarter and about 10KM west of Janakpur Road aka Pupri. The village is connected with Road from Sitamarhi and Pupri. The nearest railway station is Bajpatti(about 6km) and the nearest airport is Patna(about 150KM). The pin code is 843333. At present the Mukhia of the village is Awadh Bihari Singh. The village has a middle school and the education is available up to 8th standard. This village has some very good and educated people like Ramashray Prasad Singh, Chandravanshi Mishra etc.
