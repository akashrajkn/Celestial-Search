Klawock Seaplane Base (ICAO: PAQC, FAA LID: AQC) is located in Klawock, a city in the Prince of Wales-Outer Ketchikan Census Area of the U.S. state of Alaska.[1]

[edit] Facilities and aircraft

Klawock Seaplane Base has one seaplane landing area measuring 5,000 ft. x 1,000 ft. (1,524 m. x 305 m.). Two single-engine floatplanes are based in Klawock.[1]
