"I Don't Know What You Want But I Can't Give It Any More" (or sometimes shortened to "I Don't Know What You Want") is a single released by British Electronic music group Pet Shop Boys. The single, released on July 19, 1999, was released as the first single released from their 1999 album Nightlife. The single peaked at #15 on the UK Singles Chart, #2 on the US Hot Dance Club Play charts and #66 on the US Hot Singles Sales.
