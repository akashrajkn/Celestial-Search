Gradski SRC Slavija Stadium

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The pitch is being replaced at Gradski stadium, May 2004

Gradski SRC Slavija Stadium is a multi-use stadium in Istočno Sarajevo, Republika Srpska, Bosnia and Herzegovina. It is currently used mostly for football matches and is the home ground of Slavija Istočno Sarajevo. The stadium holds 6,000 people.
