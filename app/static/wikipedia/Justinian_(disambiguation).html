Justinian (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Justinian may refer to:

  • Justinian I (483-565), Byzantine Emperor; noted for his codification of Law.
  • Justinian II (669-711), Byzantine Emperor; also known as Rhinotmetus.
  • St. Lawrence Justinian
  • Justinian of Ramsey Island (Jestin, Iestin)
  • The Venetian family Giustiniani
  • Justinian, a storeship sent to the convict settlement at New South Wales in 1790.
