Graham Creighton Junior High School is a Halifax Regional Municipality public school that resides in Cherrybrook , Nova Scotia. After graduating from grade 6, students from Bell Park Academic Center, Joseph Giles Elementary and Humber Park Elementary will continue their studies here. It was built as a high school in 1962, but became a Junior High in 1980.
