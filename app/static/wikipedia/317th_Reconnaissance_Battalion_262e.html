317th Reconnaissance Battalion

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The 317th Reconnaissance Battalion "Vlădeasa" (Batalionul 317 Cercetare) is a reconnaissance battalion of the Romanian Land Forces, currently subordinated to the 4th Territorial Army Corps. It was formed in 1960s during the communist regime.
