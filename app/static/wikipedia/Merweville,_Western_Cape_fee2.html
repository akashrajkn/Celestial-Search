Merweville, Western Cape

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Merweville is a town located in Beaufort West Municipality, Western Cape. The town is an isolated community that maintains an old-world charm, about 150 km from Beaufort West. The surrounding region has been likened to that of Nevada or Arizona. It is one of the few towns or villages in the province not to be serviced by a sealed highway.
