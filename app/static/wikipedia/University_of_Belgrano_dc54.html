Belgrano University (Spanish: Universidad de Belgrano) was established in 1964, and it is located in the city of Buenos Aires, in the Belgrano neighborhood.

It has 9 Departments:

  • Architecture and Urban Planning
  • Law and Political Science
  • Economics
  • Humanities
  • Engineering and Computer Technology
  • Agricultural Sciences
  • Language and Foreign Studies
  • Health Sciences
  • Applied Sciences
