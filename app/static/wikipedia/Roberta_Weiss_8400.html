Roberta Weiss

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Roberta Weiss (born September 5, 1958 in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada) is a Canadian actress most notably recognized for her role as Flame Beaufort on NBC's soap opera Santa Barbara. She portrayed the role from 1990 to 1991. She was married to the show's co-star Roscoe Born from 1994 to 2000. They have 1 child together.
