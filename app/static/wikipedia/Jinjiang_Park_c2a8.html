Jinjiang Park

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other uses, see Jinjiang Park (Shanghai Metro).

Jinjiang Park (锦江乐园) is a large amusement park in Shanghai, China. Founded in 1984, it is located in Meilong district in the south-west of the city.

[edit] Transportation

Jinjiang Park can be reached by taking Shanghai Metro Line 1 to Jinjiang Park station.
