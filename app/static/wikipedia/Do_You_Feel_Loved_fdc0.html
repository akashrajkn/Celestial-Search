"Do You Feel Loved" is the second track from U2's 1997 album, Pop. In reference to the title of the song, Bono said,

"It's quite a question, but there's no question mark on it. We took the question mark out because we thought it was a bit heavy with the question mark."[1]

The song was only played during the first six PopMart Tour shows before it was dropped from the setlist, and has not been played since. The instrumental part of the song shares a striking resemblance to Naked Funk's "Alien Groove Sensation"; the album liner notes list "Do You Feel Loved" as being "inspired by Naked Funk."
