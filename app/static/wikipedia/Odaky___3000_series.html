The 3000 series (3000形, Sanzen gata?) is a commuter EMU operating on Odakyū Electric Railway in Japan. First introduced into service on 10 February 2002, a total of 312 cars were built in 8 batches with orders divided between Nippon Sharyo, Tokyu Car Corp and Kawasaki Heavy Industries. In revenue service it runs in either 6 or 8 car formations.
