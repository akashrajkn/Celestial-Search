Marengo, Saskatchewan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Marengo is a village in west central Saskatchewan, Canada, about 45 km west of Kindersley. It is located within the Sun West School Division.


According to the Canada 2001 Census:

Population: 47 (-23.0% from 1996)
Land area: 0.87 km²
Population density: 53.9 people/km²
Median age: 48.8 (males: 48.8, females: 43.0)
Total private dwellings: 33
Mean household income: $NA
