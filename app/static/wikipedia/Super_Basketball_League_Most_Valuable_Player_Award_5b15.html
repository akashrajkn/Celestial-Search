Super Basketball League Most Valuable Player Award

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Super Basketball League first named a Most Valuable Player after the 2003-04 season. The winner is chosen by reporters.

[edit] List of recipients

Year Recipient Team
2003-04 Tien Lei Dacin Tigers
2004-05 Tien Lei Dacin Tigers
2005-06 Tien Lei Dacin Tigers
2006-07 Lin Chih-chieh Taiwan Beer
