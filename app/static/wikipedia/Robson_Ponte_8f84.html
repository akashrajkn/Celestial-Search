Robson Ponte (born November 6, 1976 in São Paulo) is a Brazilian football player currently playing for Urawa Red Diamonds. Although Ponte is born in Brazil, he also holds Italian nationality. He is an all-around midfielder, and takes both penalty kicks and corners for the Reds.
