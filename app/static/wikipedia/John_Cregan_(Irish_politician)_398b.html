John Cregan (Irish politician)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For other persons named John Cregan, see John Cregan (disambiguation).

John Cregan (Irish: Seán Ó Criogáin; born 21 May 1961) is an Irish Fianna Fáil politician. He is a Teachta Dála (TD) for the constituency of Limerick West. He is a native of Dromcolliher, County Limerick.

In 1998, Cregan was elected to the 21st Seanad Éireann on the Labour Panel, in a by-election to fill the seat vacated by the Labour Party senator Seán Ryan, who had been elected to Dáil Éireann in by-election.

Cregan was first elected to Dáil Éireann in the 2002 general election and was re-elected in the 2007 general election.
