Rod Schutt

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Rod Schutt (born October 13, 1956 in Bancroft, Ontario) is a retired professional ice hockey player who played 286 games in the National Hockey League. He would with the Toronto Maple Leafs, Montreal Canadiens, and Pittsburgh Penguins.
