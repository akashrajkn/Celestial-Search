Inside Bad Company 1974-1982 is a documentary of the rock band Bad Company released in 2005. The DVD reviews Bad Company on stage, on film and on record, with rare film never previously available. Founding member Simon Kirke and biographer Steven Rosen revisit on the life and work of the classic era of Bad Company. The DVD covers the band in the 70s, 80s of the original line up of Paul Rodgers, Mick Ralphs, Boz Burrell and Simon Kirke . Featured are some of the band's biggest hits, including "Cant Get Enough," "Feel like Makin' Love," and of course, "Bad Company." but no complete performances of any songs are shown, only clips of them. The documentary has interview segments with Simon Kirke (the only member to be with the band for its entire history) and has instrumental demonstrations showing how the songs were written and played.
