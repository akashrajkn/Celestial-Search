Cornelia is an underground station on Line A of the Rome Metro. It can be found at the junction of via di Boccea and the Cornelia ring road - from which it takes its name. The station was inaugurated on the 1st of January 2000.

[edit] Services

This station has:

  • Access for the disabled
  • Escalators
  • Bus terminus
