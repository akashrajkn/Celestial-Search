Aaron Chalmers is an English association footballer who currently plays with Oldham Athletic in League One. His predominant position is as a midfielder.

Chalmers senior squad debut came on 12 April 2008 in a 2–0 win versus Leyton Orient,[1] coming on as a substitute in the 74th minute for Matthew Wolfenden.
