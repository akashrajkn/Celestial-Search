Maikel Visser

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Maikel Visser (born 3 January 1987, Enschede) is a Dutch footballer who currently plays for FC Twente. Visser is a goalkeeper who is part of the first team of FC Twente, but has yet to make his debut in professional football. He was part of the Dutch national under 19 squad.
