Sveadal, California

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sveadal is a private Swedish American community in unincorporated Santa Clara County, California. It is located in the eastern Santa Cruz Mountains, adjacent to Uvas Canyon County Park. It has been owned by the Swedish American Patriotic League since its founding in 1926. The ZIP Code is 95141 and the community is inside area code 408.
