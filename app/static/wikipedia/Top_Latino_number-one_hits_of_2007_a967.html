[edit] 2007 factoids

  • "Tu Recuerdo" by Ricky Martin featuring La Mari and Tommy Torres was the most successful song of the year.
  • "Umbrella" by Rihanna featuring Jay-Z was the most successful non-Spanish song of the year, peaking at #8 in the Year-End Chart. The second most successful non-Spanish song of the year was Beyoncé and Shakira's "Beautiful Liar", ranked at #12.
