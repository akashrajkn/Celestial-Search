OnStage

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

OnStage is a Canadian radio program, which airs Sunday afternoons on CBC Radio Two and Sunday evenings on CBC Radio One. Hosted by Shelley Solmes, the program airs classical and jazz concert performances by Canadian and international artists.
