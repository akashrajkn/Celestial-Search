College Hill Town Hall (Cincinnati, Ohio)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

College Hill Town Hall
U.S. National Register of Historic Places
Location: Cincinnati, Ohio
Architect: Samuel Hannaford [1]
Architectural style(s): Greek Revival and Renaissance[1]
Added to NRHP: March 17, 1978[1]
NRHP Reference#: 78002075[1]

College Hill Town Hall is a registered historic building in Cincinnati, Ohio, listed in the National Register on March 17, 1978.

[edit] Historic uses

  • City Hall
