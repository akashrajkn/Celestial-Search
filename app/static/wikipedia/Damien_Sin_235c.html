Damien Sin is a Singaporean author. He has written several books published by the Angsana Books imprint of Flame of the Forest publishers:

  • Classic Singapore Horror Stories, a series of horror compilations that now encompasses four volumes. The first volume was published in 1992.
  • Tall Tales and Short Stories (1995)
  • Saints, Sinners and Singaporeans: A Collection of Poems

Sin was also the frontman of the band, Fairweather Friends, whose music appeared on various Pony Canyon compilations released in Singapore in the 1990s.[citation needed] As a solo singer-songwriter he has performed at the The Substation in Singapore under the name 'The Hardcore Troubadour'.[citation needed]
