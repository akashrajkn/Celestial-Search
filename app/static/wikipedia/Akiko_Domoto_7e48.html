Akiko Domoto

Akiko Domoto (堂本暁子, Dōmoto Akiko?) (born July 31, 1932) is the governor of Chiba Prefecture in Japan. She is the first woman governor of Chiba and the third in Japanese history. She was first elected in 2001.
