State Route 204
Length: 37 mi[1] (60 km)
West end: U.S. 280/S.R. 30, just east of Pembroke
East end: I-16 exit 165 in Downtown Savannah

Branches of SR 204: 204 Spur

State Route 204 begins just east of Pembroke at U.S. 280/S.R. 30 and ends in Savannah at I-16 exit 165 (37th Street).

In Savannah, this is a very major and heavily traveled surface arterial and is known as Abercorn Expressway, Abercorn Street and 37th Street. The portion of the route running along Abercorn Street was previously numbered as State Route 359. [2]
