Masumud Dowla

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Masumud Dowla is a First class and List A cricketer from Bangladesh. He was born on 26 October 1975 in Chittagong and is a right handed batsman and off break bowler who has represented Chittagong Division between 2001/02 through 2006/07.

He has scored 8 first class fifties and 5 more in the one day game. His best first class knock, of 95, was scored against Rajshahi Division while his best one day score, of 81 came at the expense of the Sylhet Division attack. His best one day bowling was a fine spell of 4 for 17 against Rajshahi Division.
