Celaenorrhinus pyrrha,[1] commonly called Double Spotted Flat,[2] is a hesperiid butterfly which is found in South Asia.

[edit] Range

The butterfly occurs in India, Bhutan, Myanmar, Laos, and the Malay peninsula.[2] In India, the butterfly ranges from Kumaon (Uttaranchal) to Sikkim, Bhutan and eastwards towards Myanmar.[2][3]

[edit] Status

Not Rare.[3]
