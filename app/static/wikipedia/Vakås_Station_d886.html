Vakås Station (Norwegian: Vakås holdeplass) is a railway station located at Vakås in Asker, Norway on Drammenbanen. The station is served by the Oslo Commuter Rail line 400 operated by Norges Statsbaner with two hourly services. The station was opened in 1957.
