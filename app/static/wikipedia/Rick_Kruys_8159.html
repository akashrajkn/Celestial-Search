Rick Kruys

Rick Kruys (Dutch spelling Rick Kruijs) (born May 9, 1985 in Utrecht) is an footballer from the Netherlands, who plays as an attacking midfielder for FC Utrecht in the Netherlands.

He wears the shirt number 21 for his club and is regarded as one of the great new prospects for the future. He has already represented the Netherlands at the Under-21 level. His father Gert Kruys was a professional football player as well, and is currently working as a manager in Dutch football.
