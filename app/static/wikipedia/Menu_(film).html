Menu is a 1933 short comedy film directed by Nick Grinde. It was nominated for an Academy Award in 1933 for Best Short Subject (Novelty). [1]

[edit] Cast

  • Pete Smith - Narrator (voice)
  • Luis Alberni - The Master Chef (uncredited)
  • Una Merkel - Mrs. Omsk (uncredited)
  • Franklin Pangborn - John Xavier Omsk (uncredited)
