Sivan Klein (born July 8, 1984) is an Israeli beauty queen who represented her country at the Miss Universe 2003 pageant in Panama City, Panama.

[edit] Israel's Beauty Queen 2003

Sivan Klein joined the Miss Israel Pageant in 2003, in which she won the most-coveted title Israel's Beauty Queen, replacing last year's winner Yamit Har-noy. She won the rights to represent Israel at the Miss Universe 2003 pageant in Panama.

[edit] Miss Universe 2003

Sivan Klein flew to Panama for the Miss Universe 2003 pageant. She was a bet to be in the Top 15. However, she did not make it into the cut. She was 19 years old when she represented Israel in the pageant.
