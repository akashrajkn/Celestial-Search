Stefanaconi (Greek: Stephanikonion) is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Vibo Valentia in the Italian region Calabria, located about 50 km southwest of Catanzaro and about 3 km east of Vibo Valentia. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 2,477 and an area of 23.2 km².[1]

The municipality of Stefanaconi contains the frazione (subdivision) Morsillara.

Stefanaconi borders the following municipalities: Francica, Gerocarne, Pizzoni, Sant'Onofrio, Soriano Calabro, Vazzano, Vibo Valentia.
