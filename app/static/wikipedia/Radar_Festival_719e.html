Radar Festival

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Radar Festival is a music festival held annually in Croatia since 2007.

[edit] 2007 festival

The festival was held in the Siget neighbourhood of Zagreb. The festival was headlined by Kaiser Chiefs, Queens of the Stone Age and Placebo.[1] The groups Howling Bells, Anavrin and Overflow also performed.

[edit] 2008 festival

The second edition of the festival will be held in Varaždin.[2] Bob Dylan is confirmed to be performing for the festival at Varteks Stadium.[3]
