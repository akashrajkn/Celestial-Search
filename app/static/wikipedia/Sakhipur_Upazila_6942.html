Sakhipur (Bengali: সখিপুর) is an Upazila of Tangail District in the Division of Dhaka, Bangladesh.

[edit] Geography

Sakhipur is located at 24.3167° N 90.1750° E . It has 44314 units of house hold and total area 429.63 km².

[edit] Demographics

As of 1991 Bangladesh census[1], Sakhipur has a population of 220281. Males constitute are 51.01% of the population, and females 48.99%. This Upazila's eighteen up population is 116260. Sakhipur has an average literacy rate of 21.7% (7+ years), and the national average of 32.4% literate. [2]

[edit] Administrative

Sakhipur has 6 Unions, 1 pouroshova, 61 Mauzas/Mahallas, and 123 villages.
