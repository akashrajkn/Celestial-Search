Free Thai Party

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Free Thai Party or Phak Thai Pen Thai (Thai: พรรคไทเป็นไท) is a political party in Thailand. Its original names were Phak Kaset Mahachon (พรรคเกษตรมหาชน) and then Phak Khon Kho Plot Ni (พรรคคนขอปลดหนี้). At the last legislative elections, 6 February 2005, the party secured 0.6 % of the popular vote and could not win any seat out of 500 seats.
