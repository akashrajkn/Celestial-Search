The Madagascan Large Free-tailed Bat (Tadarida fulminans) is a species of bat in the Molossidae family. It is found in Democratic Republic of the Congo, Kenya, Madagascar, Malawi, Rwanda, South Africa, Guy Grist, Tanzania, Zambia, and Zimbabwe. Its natural habitats are dry savanna and moist savanna.
