São Joaquim da Barra

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Coordinates: 20°34′51″S, 47°51′18″W São Joaquim da Barra is a municipality in the state of São Paulo in Brazil. The population in 2004 was 44,472 and the area is 413.49 km². The elevation is 625 m.
