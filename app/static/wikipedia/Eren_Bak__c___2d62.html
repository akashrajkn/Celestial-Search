Eren Bakıcı, is a Turkish singer who rose to fame as one of the members of the pop group Hepsi.

[edit] Biography

Eren Bakıcı was born in Istanbul in 1984. She is the eldest of the group. Eren loves to do yoga and live a healthy lifestyle, this is made fun of in the groups T.V show, "Hepsi 1" as the other girls can't stand her cooking as it always healthy and as she never serves junk food.[1] The colour in which she appears in the groups music videos is green. She studied Ballet at university and continues to study modern dance with group member Gülçin Ergül.
