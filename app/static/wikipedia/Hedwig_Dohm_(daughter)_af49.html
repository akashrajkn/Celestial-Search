Hedwig Dohm (daughter)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Gertrud Hedwig Anna Dohm, later G. Hedwig A. Pringsheim (1855 - 1942) was a German actress.

She was the daughter of Ernst Dohm and Hedwig Dohm-Schleh, who were Jewish converts to Christianity. She married Alfred Pringsheim. They had Klaus Pringsheim, Sr. and Katharina Mann.
