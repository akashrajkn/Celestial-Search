A Work in Progress

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Work in Progress
Video by Neil Peart
Released April 23, 2002
Recorded 1997
Genre Progressive rock
Label Warner Brothers

A Work in Progress, 2-DVD box-set, documents the "work in progress" of recording Rush's Test for Echo album, as well as Neil Peart in the studio. Neil's special approach to drums is featured in songs from Test, also a 4 camera shoot as Neil lays down the fiery creative drumming for which he is known. Other topics include a discussion of Neil's DW drumset, his approach to odd times, playing with a vocalist & a "guided tour" of Neil's warmup routine.
