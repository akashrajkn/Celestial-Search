Inn (district of Switzerland)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the region in Austria, see Innviertel

Inn is an administrative district in the canton of Graubünden, Switzerland. It has an area of 1,196.77 km² and a population of 9,371 (as of December 2004).

It consists of four Kreise (sub-districts) and eighteen municipalities:
