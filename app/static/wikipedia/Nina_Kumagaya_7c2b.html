Nina Kumagaya

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Nina Kumagaya (熊谷 ニーナ, Kumagaya Nīna?, born July 18, 1963 in Osaka Prefecture) is a seiyū who is affiliated with 81 Produce.
