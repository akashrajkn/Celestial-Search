April 13 (Eastern Orthodox liturgics)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Apr. 12 - Eastern Orthodox Church calendar - Apr. 14

All fixed commemorations below celebrated on Apr. 26 by Old Calendarists

[edit] Saints

  • Hieromartyr Artemon, presbyter of Laodicea in Syria
  • Martyr Crescens of Myra in Lycia
  • Woman martyr Thomais of Alexandria
  • Martyr Eleutherius of Persia
  • Martyr Zoilus of Rome
  • Martyr Demetrius of the Peloponnesus who suffered at Tripoli
  • Saint Martius, abbot, of Clermont in Gaul
  • Russian new Hieromartyr Stephen (1933)
  • Martyr Theodosius
  • Pope Martin I, confessor, of Rome
