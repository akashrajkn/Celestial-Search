Enneanectes is a genus of triplefin fish in the family Tripterygiidae.

[edit] Species

  • Lofty triplefin, Enneanectes altivelis Rosenblatt, 1960
  • Blackedge triplefin, Enneanectes atrorus Rosenblatt, 1960
  • Roughhead triplefin, Enneanectes boehlkei Rosenblatt, 1960
  • Carmine triplefin, Enneanectes carminalis (Jordan & Gilbert, 1882)
  • Mimic triplefin, Enneanectes jordani (Evermann & Marsh, 1899)
  • Redeye triplefin, Enneanectes pectoralis (Fowler, 1941)
  • Network triplefin, Enneanectes reticulatus Allen & Robertson, 1991
  • Enneanectes smithi Lubbock & Edwards, 1981.
