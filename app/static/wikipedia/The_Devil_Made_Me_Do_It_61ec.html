The Devil Made Me Do It was the debut 1990 album of militant Black nationalist rapper Paris. MTV banned the title track. The 13 track vinyl record version of 'The Devil Made Me Do It' was released spring 1988 on Tommy Boy Records. This record is considered a collectable. Paris also toured the USA and Europe that year to promote the record.
