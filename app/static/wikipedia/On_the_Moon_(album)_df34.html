On the Moon is an album by jazz pianist, singer and composer Peter Cincotti, released on the Concord label.

[edit] Personnel

  • Peter Cincotti (piano, vocal)
  • Barak Mori (bass)
  • Mark McLean (drums)
  • Kenny Washington (drums)
  • Jeffrey Mironov (guitar)
  • Scott Kreitzer (tenor sax)
  • Sam Yahel (keyboard)
  • Wycliffe Gordon (trombone)
  • Bashiri Johnson (percussion)
  • William Galison (harmonica)
  • Brad Leali (alto sax)
  • Gary Smulyan (baritone sax)
  • Barry Danelian (trumpet)

  • Elena Barere
