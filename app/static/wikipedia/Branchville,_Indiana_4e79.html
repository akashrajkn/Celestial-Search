Branchville (also Oil Creek) is an unincorporated community in southern Oil Township, Perry County, Indiana, United States. It lies along CR40 northeast of the city of Tell City, the county seat of Perry County.[2] Its elevation is 433 feet (132 m), and it is located at 38°9′49″N, 86°34′47″W (38.1636715, -86.5797058).[3] Although Branchville is unincorporated, it has a post office, with the ZIP code of 47514.[4]
