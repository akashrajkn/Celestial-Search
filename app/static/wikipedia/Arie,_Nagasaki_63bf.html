Arie, Nagasaki

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arie (有家町; -chou) was a town located in Minamitakaki District, Nagasaki, Japan. On March 31, 2006 it merged together with several other towns into the new city of Minamishimabara.

As of 2003, the town has an estimated population of 8,990 and a density of 385.18 persons per km². The total area is 23.34 km².
