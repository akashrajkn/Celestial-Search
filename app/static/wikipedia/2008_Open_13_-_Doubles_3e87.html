2008 Open 13 - Doubles

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Main article: 2008 Open 13

Arnaud Clement and Michael Llodra were the defending champions, but withdrew to due a left ankle injury for Clement.

Martin Damm and Pavel Vizner won in the final 7–6(0), 7–5, against Yves Allegro and Jeff Coetzee.
