Stephen Hoiles (born 13 October 1981) is an Australian rugby union footballer. He currently plays for the Brumbies in the international Super 14 competition. Hoiles previously played for the New South Wales Waratahs. He has also played for the national team, the Wallabies.

Hoiles represented Australia in sevens rugby in 2001, and has also played for Australia A. He made his fully Wallaby debut in a 2004 Test match against Scotland. He has recently been named in the squad for the 2006 Wallaby Spring Tour.
