Lucas Armando Mareque Buccolini also known as Lucas Mareque is an Argentine football player. He currently plays for Independiente of the Argentine First Division.

[edit] Footballing career

Lucas was born in Morón near Buenos Aires on January 12, 1983. He is a left-back defender that rose through the ranks of River Plate.

On January 26 2007 he made his Liga Bwin debut with Porto against União de Leiria. during his time at Porto the club won the Portuguese Liga.

After playing only four games for Porto Mareque returned to Argentina to play for Independiente on loan during the Apertura 2007 tournament. In the beginning of 2008, Mareque was bought by Independiente.
