Congregation of Marian Fathers

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Fr. Stanislaus Papczynski, Founder

The Congregation of Marian Fathers of the Immaculate Conception of the Most Blessed Virgin Mary is a male clerical religious congregation founded, 1673, in Poland.
