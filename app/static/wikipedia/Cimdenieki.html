Cimdenieki

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Cimdenieki is a small suburban settlement near Liepāja, Latvia.[1] The river Ālande flows through Cimdenieki.

Cimdenieki is mostly known for its proximity to Liepāja International Airport.
