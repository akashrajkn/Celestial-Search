"Truth" is Yuna Ito's 6th single and last of 2006. This is her second single to be a part of the NANA franchise, the first being "ENDLESS STORY".

[edit] Overview

Truth was a sweepting ballad, making it her first ballad since Precious which was released on May 3, 2006. This single will be the ending theme to the movie NANA II, a movie which stars Mika Nakashima and Yuna Ito.

The PV for "Truth" was filmed in Scotland as well as the performance to the b-side song Take Me Away that was used in the movie.
