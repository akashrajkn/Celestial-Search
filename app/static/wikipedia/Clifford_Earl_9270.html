Clifford Earl
Born 28 August 1930 (1930-08-28) (age 77)
Occupation Actor


Clifford Earl (born 28 August 1930) is an English actor who has appeared in numerous television programmes and films. He appeared in the TV series Doctor Who twice, as the Station Sergeant in The Daleks' Master Plan in 1965, and as Major Branwell in The Invasion in 1968.
