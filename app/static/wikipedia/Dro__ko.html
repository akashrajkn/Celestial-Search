Drożko

Drożko[1] or Drażko (Thrasuco, Thrasucho, Thrasco, Thasco, Drasco, Drasocus) (? - 808 Rerik) - kniaź (ruler) of the Obotrites (Slavic peoples related to the Wends) in the 9th century.

  • 798 - Drożko defeated Saxons in the battle under Święciana
  • 808 - Drożko was killed by Gudfred in Reric
