Marc Didden

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Marc Didden (born July 28, 1949 in Hamont, Flanders ) is a Belgian film director. He and his family moved to Brussels when he was age two, where he had lived for most of his own life. This is also where he studied Film Direction and Playwriting.
