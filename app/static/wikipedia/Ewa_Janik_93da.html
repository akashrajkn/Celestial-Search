born 1948
Function: member of Sejm 2005-2007
Party: Sojusz Lewicy Demokratycznej

Ewa Janik (born December 19, 1948 in Częstochowa) is a Polish politician. She was elected to Sejm on September 25, 2005 getting 6077 votes in 28 Częstochowa district, candidating from Sojusz Lewicy Demokratycznej list.

She was also a member of Sejm 1997-2001 and Sejm 2001-2005.
