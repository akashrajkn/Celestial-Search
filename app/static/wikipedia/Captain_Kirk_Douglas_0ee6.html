"Captain" Kirk Douglas

"Captain" Kirk Douglas is an American guitarist who plays with the hip hop band The Roots. He joined the band in 2003. Douglas also has a rock & roll band in the works. He has performed with Dave Matthews Band on at least three occasions.

New York Times, "Rolling Across the Decades With Dylan," by Jon Pareles. Paragraph on the Roots mentions Douglas

He is endorsed by Mesa Boogie amplifiers and uses the Stiletto series amps for his sound.
