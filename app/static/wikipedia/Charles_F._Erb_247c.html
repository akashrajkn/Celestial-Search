Charles F. Erb

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Charles F. Erb was a college football head coach at Idaho, and Nevada. In 1924, he coached at Nevada, where he compiled a 3-4-1 record. From 1926 to 1928, he coached at Idaho, where he compiled a 10-9-1 record. Overall, his career record stands at 13-13-1. In 1935, he coached at Humboldt State, where he compiled a 6-1-1 record. He played college football at Cal.
