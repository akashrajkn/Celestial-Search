Pir Panjal Range

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Pir Panjal Range is a range of mountains that form a part of the Middle Himalayas across the state of Himachal Pradesh in the Republic of India as well as Pakistan-administered Kashmir and Indian Occupied Kashmir. The well known hill stations of Gulmarg and Pahalgam are present in this range.
