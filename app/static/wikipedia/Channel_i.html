Channel i

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Channel i is a privately owned Television network in Bangladesh. It currently broadcasts satellite transmissions using PanAm Sat, which covers most of Asia and parts of Australia and Europe where it has started broadcasting in October, 2007 on Sky EPG channel 826. It is owned by the Impress group and started transmissions on October 1, 1999.
