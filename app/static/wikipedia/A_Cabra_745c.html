A Cabra

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A Cabra (full name: A Cabra - Jornal Universitário de Coimbra) is a newspaper published in Coimbra, Portugal, by the Associação Académica de Coimbra (Coimbra Academic Association), which is the students' union of the University of Coimbra.
