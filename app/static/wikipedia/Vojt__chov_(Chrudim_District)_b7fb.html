Vojtěchov (Chrudim District)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Vojtěchov in the Czech Republic
See other places named Vojtěchov.

Vojtěchov is a village in the Pardubice Region of the Czech Republic. It has around 420 inhabitants.

Village Pláňavy is administrative part of Vojtěchov.
