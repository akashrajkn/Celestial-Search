Eliomys is a genus of rodent in the Gliridae family. It contains the following species:

  • Asian Garden Dormouse (Eliomys melanurus)
  • Maghreb Garden Dormouse (Eliomys munbyanus)
  • Garden dormouse (Eliomys quercinus)
