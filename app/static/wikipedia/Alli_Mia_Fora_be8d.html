Alli Mia Fora (Greek: Άλλη μια φορά; English: One More Time) is the name of an album by Greek musical group Antique. The album was released in 2003 by V2 Records and it became gold in Greece, their first album to do so. In 2004, many of the songs from this album were included in English on their Swedish release titled Blue Love.
