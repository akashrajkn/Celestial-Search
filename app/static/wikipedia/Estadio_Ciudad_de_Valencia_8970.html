Estadio Ciudad de Valencia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Estadio Ciudad de Valencia (City of Valencia Stadium) is a football stadium in Valencia, Spain, and is the home ground of Levante UD. The stadium holds 25,400 and was built in 1969.
