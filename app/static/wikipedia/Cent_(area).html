Cent (area)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

In India, Cent is a measure of area.

1 Cent = 435.6 sq. ft.

1 Acre = 100 Cents

1 Acre = 18.15 Ground

1 Ground = 2400 sq.ft
