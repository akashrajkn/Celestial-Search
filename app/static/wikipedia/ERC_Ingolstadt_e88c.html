ERC Ingolstadt

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The ERC Ingolstadt (Eishockey-und-Rollschuh club) are a member of the Deutsche Eishockey-Liga. Commonly known as the Panthers, they play their games in Ingolstadt, Germany at the Saturn Arena. During the 2004-05 NHL lockout, Ingolstadt signed NHLers Marco Sturm, Andy McDonald and Jamie Langenbrunner.
