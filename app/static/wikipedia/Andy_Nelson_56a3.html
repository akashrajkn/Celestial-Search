Andrew Vaughan Nelson (born May 27, 1933 in Athens, Alabama) was an American football safety in the NFL for the Baltimore Colts and New York Giants. He attended the University of Memphis. He currently owns and runs a small BBQ establishment, Andy Nelson's Barbecue.
