Divisions of Uttar Pradesh

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Uttar Pradesh, one of the states of India, is divided into following administrative geographical divisions, each consisting of more than one district:

  • Agra Division
  • Aligarh Division
  • Allahabad Division
  • Azamgarh Division
  • Bareilly Division
  • Basti Division
  • Chitrakoot Division
  • Devipatan Division
  • Faizabad Division
  • Gorakhpur Division
  • Jhansi Division
  • Kanpur Division
  • Lucknow Division
  • Mirzapur Division
  • Moradabad Division
  • Saharanpur Division
  • Varanasi Division
