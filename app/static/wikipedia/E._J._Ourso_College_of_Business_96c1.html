E. J. Ourso College of Business
Established: 1997[1]
Type: Public
Dean: Eli Jones[2]
Location: Baton Rouge, LA, USA
Website: http://www.bus.lsu.edu/

The E. J. Ourso College of Business is Louisiana State University's business school. In 1997 it was named after E. J. Ourso, the college's financial benefactor.

It is certified by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business.[3]
