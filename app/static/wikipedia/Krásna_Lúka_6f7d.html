Krásna Lúka

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Location of Sabinov District in the Prešov Region.

Krásna Lúka is a village and municipality in Sabinov District in the Prešov Region of north-eastern Slovakia.

[edit] History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1329.

[edit] Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 561 metres and covers an area of 10.352 km². It has a population of about 720 people.
