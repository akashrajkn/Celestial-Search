Canada Bay, Newfoundland and Labrador

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia


Canada Bay
Location of Canada Bay in Newfoundland

Canada Bay, a natural bay located on the east coast of the Great Northern Peninsula, Newfoundland, Canada is home to some spectacular scenery is divided into two narrow indrafts, Chimney Bay and Bide Arm. The entrance to Canada Bay is between Canada Head ( 50°43′11″N, 56°06′47″W) and Cape Daumalen ( 50°41′29″N, 56°06′50″W). Coordinates: 50.72° N 56.15° W
