Guča (Serbian Cyrillic: Гуча) is a small town in Serbia. It is situated in the Lučani municipality, Moravica District. The population of the town is 2,022 people. It is famous for its yearly trumpet festival, attended by several hundred thousand visitors each year.

[edit] Future Developmant

JAT Airways are planning on building a heliport for the festival.
