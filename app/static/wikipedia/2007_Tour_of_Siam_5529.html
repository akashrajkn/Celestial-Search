2007 Tour of Siam

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The 2007 Tour of Siam was held from 20 to 25 January 2007 in Thailand. It was a multiple stage road cycling race that took part over six stages with a total of 961.8 kilometres and is part of the 2006-2007 UCI Asia Tour.
