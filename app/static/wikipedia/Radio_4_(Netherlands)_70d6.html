Radio 4 is a Dutch NPO radio station traditional music. Radio 4 began broadcasting in December 1975 under the name Hilversum 4. The first transmission was Veronica. On November 2004 the following broadcastings air on Radio 4: TROS, AVRO, NPS, VARA, NCRV, VPRO, KRO.
