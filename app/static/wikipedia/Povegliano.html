Povegliano is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Treviso in the Italian region Veneto, located about 40 km north of Venice and about 12 km northwest of Treviso. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 4,590 and an area of 12.9 km².[1]

The municipality of Povegliano contains the frazioni (subdivisions, mainly villages and hamlets) Camalò and Santandrà.

Povegliano borders the following municipalities: Arcade, Giavera del Montello, Ponzano Veneto, Villorba, Volpago del Montello.
