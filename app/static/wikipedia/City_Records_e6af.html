City Records

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

City Records (full legal name: Preduzeće za izdavačko-propagandno delatnost City Records d.o.o. Beograd) is a Serbian record label.

Founded in 1997, it is one of the most successful record labels in Southeastern Europe. The record label is part of the Pink Media Group, which also owns RTV Pink. It's the home of some of the best selling Balkans folk, pop-folk, and turbo-folk artists.

Registered as a limited liability company, it has 14 full-time employees.[1]

City Records has signed licensing agreements with many European artists, increasing the exposure of these artists and the distribution of their musical work in the market.
