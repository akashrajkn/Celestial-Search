Main article: Swimming at the 2007 World Aquatics Championships

The Women's 100 metres Butterfly event was the 1st event contested on the 2007 World Aquatics Championship for Swimming.

The 10 Heats began at 10:00, on March 25, 2007, at the Rod Laver Arena, in Melbourne Park.

The semifinals started on the evening of the same day at 19:00.

The Final started at 19:12 on the following day, March 26.
