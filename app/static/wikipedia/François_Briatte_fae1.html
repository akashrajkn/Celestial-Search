François Briatte

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

François Briatte (September 27, 1805 - January 30, 1877) was a Swiss politician, member of the Conseil d'Etat of the Canton of Vaud (1845-1861), member and several times President of the Swiss Council of States.
