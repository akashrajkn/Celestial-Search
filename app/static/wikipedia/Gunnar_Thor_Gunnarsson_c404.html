Gunnar Þor Gunnarsson (born 4 October 1985) is an Icelandic football player who currently represents IFK Norrköping in Allsvenskan. He left Hammarby IF on the 1 November 2007, signed a 3-year contract with "Peking".

He signed Hammarby IF before the 2006 season, leaving Fram. He is a defender who plays most commonly at left back.

[edit] International career

His first international appearance for Iceland was against Spain in March 28, 2007.
