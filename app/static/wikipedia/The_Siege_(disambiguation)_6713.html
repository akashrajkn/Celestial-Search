The Siege (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Siege is a 1998 film directed by Edward Zwick and starring Denzel Washington, Bruce Willis, Annette Bening, and Tony Shalhoub.

The Siege may also refer to:

  • A Canadian action-thriller from 1983, which also is called Self Defense (1983 film).
  • Guardians of Ga'Hoole Book 4: The Siege, the fourth novel in the series entitled Guardians of Ga'hoole
  • "The Siege" (DS9 episode), the third episode of the second season of the television series Star Trek: Deep Space Nine
  • The Siege - the second novel of the Return of the Archwizards trilogy, by Troy Denning
  • "The Siege" (Stargate Atlantis), a three-part episode of the science fiction television series Stargate Atlantis
  • The Siege, a collection of stories by James Lasdun.
  • "The Siege," the title story of the previously mentioned collection, which was adapted into a film, Besieged, directed and written by Bernardo Bertolucci.
