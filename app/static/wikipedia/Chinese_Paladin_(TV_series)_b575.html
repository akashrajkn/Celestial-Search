Chinese Paladin (TV series)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Chinese Paladin (Xian Jian Qi Xia Zhuan) is a 2004 TV series based on the game The Legend of Sword and Fairy 1. It is known as Chinese Paladin in English.

[edit] Details

  • Title: 仙剑奇侠传 / 仙劍奇俠傳 / Xian Jian Qi Xia Zhuan
  • Also known as: The Magic Sword & the Chivalrous Youth / The Sword and the Fairy
  • Genres: Adventure / Fantasy / Martial Arts / Romance
  • Episodes: 38 (34 in certain TV broadcasts)
  • Broadcast network: CTV / TCS (Joint Mainland China, Taiwan and Singapore production)
  • Broadcast period: 2004
  • Opening theme song: Sha Po Lang (殺破狼)
  • Ending theme song: Zhong Yu Ming Bai (終於明白)

[edit] Cast

  • Hu Ge (胡歌) as Li Xiaoyao (李逍遥)
  • Liu Yifei (刘亦菲) as Zhao Ling'er (赵灵儿)
  • An Yixuan (安以轩) as Lin Yueru (林月如)
  • Liu Pinyan (刘品言) as A Nu (阿奴)
  • Eddie Peng Yuyan (彭于晏) as Tang Yu (唐钰)
  • Bryan Wong (王禄江) as Liu Jingyuan (刘晋元)
  • Zhen Peipei (郑佩佩) as Lao Lao (姥姥)
  • Xie Junhao (谢君豪) as Jiu Jianxian (酒剑仙)
