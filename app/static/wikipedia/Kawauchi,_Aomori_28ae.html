Kawauchi, Aomori

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about the former town in Aomori. Kawauchi also was the name of a town in Ehime, see Kawauchi, Ehime (now part of the city of Tōon, Ehime), and is the name of a village in Fukushima, see Kawauchi, Fukushima.

Kawauchi (川内町, Kawauchi-machi?) was a town located in Shimokita District, Aomori, Japan. The town was founded in 1889 from the merger of four villages:

  • Kawauchi
  • Hinokigawa
  • Shukunohe
  • Kakizaki

On March 14, 2005 Kawauchi merged into the city of Mutsu.

As of 2003, the town had an estimated population of 5,359 and a density of 16.56 persons per km². The total area was 323.67 km².
