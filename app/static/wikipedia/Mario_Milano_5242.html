Mario Milano is a former professional wrestler and a former manager for the now defunct Professional Championship Wrestling in Victoria, Australia. Milano got his start in wrestling in Caracas, Venezuela at the age of 17, wrestling over in the USA in the south as a regular Tag Team partner with Len Rossi.

He uses the finisher Atomic Drop and did an angle where used it on a 400lbs wrestler
