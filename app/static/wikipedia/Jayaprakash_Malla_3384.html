Jayaprakash Malla

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jayaprakash Malla (जयप्रकाश मल्ल) was the last king of Yen (यें) or Kantipur (कान्तिपुर). He contributed to the literature of Nepal Bhasa as well.
