Valeri Georgiev

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Valeri Georgiev (Bulgarian: Валери Георгиев) (born 28 July 1984) is a Bulgarian football player, currently playing for FC Vihren Sandanski as a defender.Georgiev is a left defender. He started your career in home town Blagoevgrad in local team Pirin.In june 2005 signed with FC Vihren Sandanski.
