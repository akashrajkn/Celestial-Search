Eleazar Gómez

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Eleazar Gómez (born May 29, 1986) is a Mexican actor known for his performance in Mexican telenovelas. Gómez is the brother of actress Zoraida Gómez and actor Jairo Gómez.
